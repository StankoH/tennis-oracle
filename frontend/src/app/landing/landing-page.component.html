What is Bonzobyte?

Bonzobyte is an independent tennis analytics platform built with one goal in mind: to provide the most detailed and accurate insights into matches, players, and performance ‚Äì all driven by real data and custom-built models.

What makes it different?

Instead of surface-level stats, Bonzobyte uses our own neural networks, Bayesian ranking models, and custom implementations of TrueSkill to generate predictive analytics and player evaluation metrics. All match data is hand-cleaned, structured and compressed for efficiency.

This project began as a passion for tennis and technology ‚Äì a hobby that grew into a serious engineering effort. Bonzobyte is not a commercial product; it's a living, evolving project that reflects our curiosity, discipline, and love for the sport.
